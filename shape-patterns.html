How to Use:

For shape patterns, you want to draw the shape in the center of the section the pattern is in, and then repeatedly outline the shape to the edges of the pattern. Then, starting from the center you fill in the shape patterns moving outward the same way you fill in the line patterns. Here are a couple of examples of completed shape patterns to illustrate what I mean:
